Operator Compliance Risk Score

Operator Compliance Risk Score (OCRS) is a mechanism used at roadside checks to calculate the likelihood of an operator being non-compliant.

It is a risk based scoring system and is used as a guide only. It is NOT a mechanism for rating operators. Seperate scores are produced for Roadworthiness and Traffic categories.
